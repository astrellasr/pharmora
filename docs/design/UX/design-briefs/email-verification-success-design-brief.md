# Email Verification Success

---

## Overview

The Email Verification Success page confirms that the user's email address has been successfully verified.

This is the final step of the account activation process before users can sign in to Pharmora.

The page should reassure users that their account is now active and guide them toward the next logical action: signing in.

The experience should feel clean, secure, trustworthy, and premium.

---

## User Goal

"My account is now verified.

Let me continue to sign in."

---

## Business Goal

Complete the account activation flow while providing a reassuring confirmation experience.
