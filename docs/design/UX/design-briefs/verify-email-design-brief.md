# Verify Email

---

## Overview

The Verify Email page is displayed immediately after a user successfully creates a new Pharmora account.

Its purpose is to inform users that a verification email has been sent to their registered email address before they can access the system.

This page should reassure users, explain the next steps clearly, and provide actions if they did not receive the email.

The overall experience should feel secure, modern, minimal, and enterprise-grade.

---

## User Goal

"I've created my account.
Now tell me exactly what I need to do next."

---

## Business Goal

Increase successful email verification while reducing confusion and unnecessary support requests.
